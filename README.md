# Real-PiCam
Raspberry PI camera to capture real moments without your phone


# Idea
I always had the idea of leaving my phone behind and going outside to do stuff, but I also want small camera to be able to capture pictures.

I found some parts laying around and decided to make a small pocket camera

## Parts

|System|Part|
|---|---|
| Logic | Raspberry PI Zero 1 |
| camera | [Waveshare RPI camera 5MP](https://erelement.com/shop/rpi-camera-d/)|
